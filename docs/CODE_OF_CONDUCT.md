# Code of Conduct
## 1. General Principles

By interacting with this repository, you agree to the following:

- **Compliance**: You must comply with the laws of your jurisdiction and the Terms of Service of the platforms you are using (like GitHub and/or etc.).

- **Common** **Decency**: Try to treat each other with basic respect.

## 2. Reality Check

This is the **internet**. Please be prepared for blunt honesty. I don't police every word, and participants may encounter strong language or unfiltered opinions. If you are easily offended, consider this a fair warning.

## 3. Maintainer’s Rights

The repository owner reserves the right, at their sole discretion and without prior explanation, to use any available platform tools (including but not limited to blocking, deleting comments, or restricting access) against contributors or any other users.

## 4. Some Piece of Philosophy

Despite the points above, the "nuclear option" is rarely used. I prefer a hands-off approach and will most likely not interfere with your activities unless it becomes absolutely necessary.
